What are my goals for this program and beyond?

I want to begin by building a career in the industry with the skills
and knowledge I have gained from Galvanize. I then want to keep learning.
I have a desire to build an online platform for screenwriters so they have
a place to work even if they live in different time zones. One of my other
passions other than filmmaking is video games. My ultimate goal is to become
a video game developer. I would love to write them. I have found that those
who write the games are most commonly the same people who code them. I want
to get to this point so that I can write and develop my own video game.
